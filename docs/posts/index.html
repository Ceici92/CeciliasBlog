Hello everyone ! After creating my blog, it is time to write my first Human Computer Interaction’s assignment. Affordances As I saw in class, an affordance is a property of an artefact which indicates its usage. To illustrate this concept, I chose to talk about my vacuum cleaner. Its handle is well done. Automatically through its form, we know how to use the vacuum. On the contrary, a bad affordance leads us to an incorrect usage of an object.
